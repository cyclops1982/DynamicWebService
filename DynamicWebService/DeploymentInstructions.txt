To deploy, build the solution and copy the DynamicWebService.dll found in
<project dir>/bin/debug/ to C:\Program Files\K2 blackpearl\ServiceBroker

Then run the BrokerManagement.exe program found in that directory.

* Select Configure Service
* Right click on the services tree item in the left pane and register a new service.
* Enter in whatever details you want for the various names, and specify the  .dll you copied as the assembly
